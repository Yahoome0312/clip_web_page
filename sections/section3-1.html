3.1 Zero-Shot Classification

This is CLIP's "magic trick". Traditional models need training for every new category. CLIP does not. It can classify images into categories it has never seen before during training.

How it works

  1. Prompt Engineering: We turn labels into sentences: "cat" → "A photo of a cat".
  2. Encoding: We encode the image and all candidate text sentences.
  3. Comparison: We calculate Cosine Similarity. The sentence with the highest score wins.
